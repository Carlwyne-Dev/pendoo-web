Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. Data Collection

Pendoo is designed with privacy as the core feature. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on any servers.

2. Local Storage

All items, notes, dates, and settings you create within Pendoo are stored 100% locally on your device. Only you have access to this information.

3. Push Notifications

Pendoo schedules local push notifications on your device for expected dates and follow-ups. These are generated locally and do not rely on remote servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Pendoo does not use third-party analytics or tracking software. The app operates entirely offline after download.
